WHY ISIELTS IMPORTANT IN YOUR IMMIGRATION JOURNEY


IELTS, the International English Language Testing System, is the bridge to
your global aspirations. It measures your English language proficiency and
is recognized by universities, employers, and immigration authorities world-
wide. Whether pursuing higher education, career advancement, or immigrat-
ion, IELTS certifies your language skills, opens doors to opportunities, and
paves the way for success.





IELTS SCOREPARAMETERS IELTS SCORE PARAMETERS


IELTS assesses your English language proficiency through four bands:
Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. The scoring parameter
ranges from 0 to 9, where 0 is awarded to the least performing and 9
is awarded to the expert. Achieving at least 6 in these bands is crucial
for securing immigration opportunities abroad.
