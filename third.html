WHY DO YOU NEED VISIT VISA


Be it discovering the world's wonders or visiting your family or conducting business, you need a
visit visa to make that happen. You get to choose between a tourist or a business visa, depending
on your purpose.



Tourist visas offer entry for leisure and sightseeing, with limited validity and no business engagement. A business visa lets you conduct client meetings, conferences, and sales deals while providing time for longer stays, establishing temporary workspaces, and exploring the country.

However, application procedures, eligibility criteria, and required documents vary across countries.


BASIC PRE-REQUISITES


A passport with a validity of at least 6 months from the travel date Passport's bio page photocopy.

Copy of the two-way tickets.


Recent bank statement as evidence of financial resources

Invitation letter from a
sponsor, friend, or family
member residing overseas


The sponsor's financial statement in the overseas country must be included

Sponsor's passport photocopy


A marriage certificate, if married to a foreign national

Parents' birth certificates &
marriage certificates (for minors)
